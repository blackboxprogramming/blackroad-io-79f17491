Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Our Core Principle

BlackRoad is designed to run entirely on your local hardware. Your AI conversations, prompts, and generated content never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them. We believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a feature.

Local Processing

BlackRoad processes all AI inference locally on your device. This means:

  • Your prompts stay local. Every message you send to the AI is processed on your hardware.
  • Your outputs stay local. All generated text, code, and content remains on your device.
  • No cloud required. BlackRoad functions fully offline after initial installation.
  • No training on your data. Your conversations are never used to train or improve AI models.

What We Collect

By default, BlackRoad collects no personal data. We do not:

  • Record your conversations or prompts
  • Track your usage patterns
  • Collect device identifiers
  • Store any data on our servers

The only network requests made by default are:

  • Model downloads: When you choose to download a model from our registry
  • Update checks: Optional version checks (can be disabled in settings)

Optional Telemetry

BlackRoad includes an optional, opt-in telemetry system that is disabled by default. If you choose to enable it, we collect:

  • Application version and operating system
  • Hardware type (CPU architecture, GPU presence)
  • Aggregate usage statistics (session count, models used)
  • Crash reports and error logs

We never collect:

  • Prompt content or generated outputs
  • Personal information or identifiers
  • IP addresses (anonymized before storage)

How to Control Telemetry

Telemetry can be enabled or disabled at any time in Settings, or via the CLI with blackroad config set telemetry.enabled false

Website Data

When you visit our website (blackroad.io), we collect minimal data:

  • Analytics: We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not track individual users or use cookies
  • Contact forms: If you contact us, we store your message and email to respond
  • Downloads: We log download counts (not individual IP addresses)

Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The only exceptions are:

  • Infrastructure providers: We use cloud services for hosting our website and model registry (Cloudflare, AWS). These services process network requests but do not have access to your local BlackRoad data.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

Since BlackRoad processes data locally and we collect minimal information by default, exercising these rights is straightforward. For any data stored locally, you have complete control through your operating system.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • For significant changes, notifying users through the application
