About BlackRoad

BlackRoad is building the infrastructure for sovereign AI—systems that run locally, respect user privacy, and remain under individual control.

We believe AI should empower individuals, not surveil them. Our tools are designed to work entirely on your hardware, with no data leaving your machine unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Our Mission

To democratize access to powerful AI capabilities while preserving individual sovereignty, privacy, and autonomy. We're creating tools that put users in complete control of their AI interactions.

Core Values

Privacy by Design

Your data never leaves your device. We build systems that work entirely offline.

User Sovereignty

You own your AI. No subscriptions, no API limits, no external dependencies.

Open Development

Transparent, auditable code. Community-driven development and governance.

Accessibility

AI for everyone, regardless of technical expertise or resources.

The Future We're Building

We envision a world where powerful AI assistants run on consumer hardware, where individuals maintain full control over their digital tools, and where privacy is the default—not a premium feature.
